Our Company – AnZ Home Furnishings

Welcome to AnZ Home Furnishings – where quality craftsmanship meets luxury. Based in Chennai, we specialize in delivering premium furnishings for both residential and commercial spaces.

We offer a wide range of products including custom sofas, curtains, blinds, wallpapers, flooring, and more. With our in-house manufacturing and tailoring units, every product is crafted to meet the highest standards.

We work closely with designers and architects to create elegant, functional interiors — making us an ideal partner for hotels, offices, and modern homes.
